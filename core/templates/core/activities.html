Instrument Petting Zoo

Member of Blair Bands and Orchestras will be on hand with their instruments so kids can see them, learn about them, and can try them out. We expect to have a full compliment of band and orchestra instruments, as well as pianos. This is something the National Symphony Orchestra does, but it costs way more and is all the way downtown!

Marching Band

The festival will also feature the MBHS Marching Band showcase and two fifteen minute performances at 10:15 and 11:30. Make sure you catch them!

Orff Instruments

Orff Group Drumming Lesson for kids 7 and up! Offered at 10:30 and 12:15.

Arts and Crafts

We will have several stations featuring coloring, word searches, origami, puzzles, and instrument making. Give your child a chance to show off their skills and encourage creativity and early learning!

Face Painting

The festival will feature face-painting, allowing your child to express their creativity in a fun and delightful manner! All materials are 100% safe and we will have trained volunteers on-hand for assistance.

Cupcake Walk

Don't miss the chance to win a cupcake! The game begins when the music starts; children walk around in a circle as the music is playing. When the music stops, the children stop. A volunteer will call out a carnival image and the child standing on the corresponding image wins a cupcake!

Raffle

The raffle will be called after each orchestra performance. Prizes may include books, instruments, music lessons, and camp registration!

Food Sale

We will have a concessions table with snacks, baked goods and drinks for sale. All proceeds go towards funding Blair Instrumental Music and help support events such as this one.
